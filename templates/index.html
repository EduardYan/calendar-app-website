What is?

Calendar Terminal App, is a application for watch calendars of whatever year or month from the terminal or console.
Current Version is 1.0, only for Linux and Mac. But comming soon for Windows.

Use.

Download the program in the section of download After you can use the program of this form.

  1. GNU/Linux and Mac OS.

    When you have the program downloaded, you can execute for see alls the help and use.
    Also you can move the program to /usr/bin. For execute without the ./

    ./calendar -h

    For see a year or month.

    ./calendar -y 2015 -m 12 ./calendar --year 2020
    --month 3

    If you can get the year current and alls the months.
    Execute easily.

    ./calendar
